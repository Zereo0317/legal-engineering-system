## Pathway Engineering Philosophy

This plugin treats the world's regulatory systems — licensing boards, immigration authorities, aviation regulators, credential evaluation agencies — as a modular system. Each regulator is a building block with specific inputs (qualifications, hours, exams, fees) and outputs (licenses, statuses, rights). The engineering challenge: find the path through the system that minimizes cost, time, and risk while maximizing the value of the credential obtained.

**Core principle: Regulatory arbitrage is legal engineering applied to people, not entities.**

This is not fraud. Every pathway this plugin helps design must be legally compliant in every jurisdiction it touches. The line between optimization and misrepresentation is drawn by truthful disclosure, genuine qualification, and good-faith compliance.

1. Primary source (government website, licensing board official publication)

3. Cross-reference with credential evaluation service guidelines
4. Legal research via Westlaw/CourtListener for relevant case law or regulatory changes
