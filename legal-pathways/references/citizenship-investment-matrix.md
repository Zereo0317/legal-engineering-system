## Due Diligence and Compliance

### What Programs Require

| Check | Typical Scope |
|---|---|
| **Source of funds** | Bank statements, business financials, tax returns |
| **Criminal background** | Interpol, FBI (US), local police clearance |
| **Sanctions screening** | OFAC, EU, UN sanctions lists |
| **PEP screening** | Politically exposed persons check |
| **Health check** | Medical exam (some programs) |
| **Interview** | In-person or video (some programs) |

### CRS/AEOI Implications

A second citizenship or residency does NOT exempt you from tax reporting obligations
in your home country. CRS (Common Reporting Standard) exchanges financial account
information between 100+ jurisdictions. Your financial accounts in any CRS jurisdiction
will be reported to your tax residence country.

**The legal engineering response**: Second citizenship is about mobility and optionality,
not tax evasion. Change your tax residence legally if you want to change where you pay tax.
