# Legal Pathways Engineering

Cross-border credential, licensing, immigration, and sovereign individual pathway
optimizer — a Claude Code plugin that treats regulatory systems as modular building
blocks for the fastest, cheapest, most legally robust pathway to your target credential,
status, or sovereign architecture.

## What this is

Legal Pathways Engineering extends the Legal Engineering discipline beyond wealth
structuring into **personal legal infrastructure**: the credentials, licenses,
certifications, citizenships, and immigration statuses that determine what you can do,
where you can live, and what opportunities you can access.

**Core principle: Regulatory arbitrage is legal engineering applied to people, not entities.**

Three properties make it engineering:
- **Repeatable** — the same pathway pattern adapts for different backgrounds
- **Optimizable** — as regulations, treaties, and mutual recognition agreements change, pathways adjust
- **Stackable** — each credential is modular; one qualification unlocks the next

**Target audience**: Elites, UHNW individuals, ambitious professionals, empire builders,
founders, and anyone who wants to become one. People who treat the world's regulatory
systems as a design space, not a constraint.

## Key Design Principles

1. **Capture enforcement reality, not just official policy.** The gap between law-on-the-books and law-in-practice is itself actionable intelligence. Singapore's "no dual citizenship" has edge cases. Taiwan's military service obligations have exceptions. The plugin surfaces both.

2. **Treat regulatory systems as building blocks.** Each licensing board, immigration authority, and aviation regulator is a module with specific inputs and outputs. The engineering challenge is finding the optimal path through the system.

3. **Optimize for the whole person, not just one dimension.** Immigration affects tax. Tax affects business structure. Business structure affects asset protection. The sovereign-architect skill integrates all dimensions.

4. **No fraud. No misrepresentation. No shortcuts that create legal risk.** Every pathway must be legally defensible in every jurisdiction it touches. The line between optimization and violation is drawn by truthful disclosure and genuine compliance.

5. **Cultural context matters.** A Taiwanese professional's path to Singapore citizenship is different from an Indian professional's. A Greater China family's sovereign architecture is different from a Gulf family's. The plugin adapts.
