# /salecraft-compliance

Reviews AI-generated marketing content (particularly from SaleCraft) for legal compliance across multiple jurisdictions. Ensures advertising claims, product descriptions, and marketing materials meet regulatory requirements before publication.

## Instructions

1. **Load practice profile.** Read `~/.claude/plugins/config/legal-engineering/cross-border-wealth/CLAUDE.md`.

2. **Identify the content and context:**
   - Content type: ad copy, landing page, social media post, email campaign, product description
   - Product/service category: health, cosmetics, food/supplement, technology, financial, general
   - Target markets: Taiwan, US, EU, or multi-jurisdiction
   - Target audience: consumers, businesses, vulnerable populations
   - Distribution channels: online, broadcast, print, social media
   - Was this generated by AI? (additional disclosure requirements may apply)

3. **Taiwan Fair Trade Act (公平交易法) review:**

   ### Prohibited practices (Arts. 21-25)
   - [ ] False or misleading representations (虛偽不實或引人錯誤之表示)
   - [ ] Unsubstantiated claims (無法證實之宣稱)
   - [ ] Comparative advertising without factual basis (不當比較廣告)
   - [ ] Deceptive packaging or labeling (欺罔之包裝標示)
   - [ ] Multi-level marketing violations (多層次傳銷違規)

   ### FTC Taiwan enforcement focus areas
   - Testimonials without substantiation
   - "Best" / "No. 1" claims without survey evidence
   - Before/after comparisons without documentation
   - Price claims (original price, discount) without basis

4. **Consumer Protection Act (消費者保護法) review:**

   ### Key requirements
   - Art. 22: Advertising content becomes part of the contract
   - Art. 22-1: Media that publishes ads may be jointly liable
   - Art. 23: Entrepreneurs liable for advertising representations
   - Art. 7: Product liability for unsafe goods/services
   - Art. 11-1: Unfair standard contract terms
   - Art. 18/19: Distance selling (7-day cooling-off period)

   ### Specific implications for AI-generated content
   - Every claim in the ad is potentially a contractual promise
   - Entrepreneur cannot disclaim advertising representations
   - AI-generated content does not reduce liability

5. **US FTC Guidelines (for US-facing content):**

   ### FTC Act Section 5 / Endorsement Guides
   - [ ] Material connections disclosed (sponsored, paid, gifted)
   - [ ] Testimonials reflect typical results (or clear disclaimer)
   - [ ] Substantiation exists for objective claims
   - [ ] Environmental claims meet Green Guides standards
   - [ ] "Made in USA" claims meet all-or-virtually-all standard
   - [ ] AI-generated content properly disclosed where required

   ### Category-specific (US)
   - Health claims: FTC Health Products Compliance Guidance
   - Financial claims: SEC advertising rules, Reg BI
   - Children: COPPA, CARU guidelines

6. **Category-specific regulations:**

   ### Health/Medical claims
   - Taiwan: 藥事法 (Pharmaceutical Affairs Act) — no medical claims without approval
   - Taiwan: 食品安全衛生管理法 — food cannot claim medical efficacy
   - Taiwan: 化粧品衛生安全管理法 — cosmetics claims restrictions
   - Permitted vs. prohibited claim language (reference MOHW guidelines)

   ### Food/Supplement claims
   - Cannot claim to cure, treat, or prevent disease
   - Health function claims require approved health food (健康食品) certification
   - Nutrient content claims must meet specific thresholds

   ### Financial services
   - Risk disclaimers required
   - Past performance disclaimers
   - License/registration number display requirements

7. **Multi-jurisdiction compliance matrix:**

   | Claim | Taiwan FTA | Taiwan CPA | US FTC | EU UCPD | Status | Fix |
   |---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
   | "Best quality" | ❌ Need survey | ⚠️ Contractual | ❌ Unsubstantiated | ❌ Misleading | 🔴 Fail | Remove or substantiate |
   | "Clinically proven" | ❌ Need study | ⚠️ Contractual | ❌ Need RCT | ❌ Need evidence | 🔴 Fail | Provide study or remove |
   | "Save 50%" | ⚠️ Need basis | ⚠️ Contractual | ⚠️ Need former price | ⚠️ Need reference | 🟡 Fix | Document original price |
   | "Made with natural ingredients" | ✅ OK if true | ⚠️ Contractual | ⚠️ Define "natural" | ⚠️ Define scope | 🟡 Fix | Specify percentage |

8. **AI-generated content disclosure:**
   - Is disclosure required that content was AI-generated?
   - Jurisdiction-specific requirements for AI disclosure
   - Recommended disclosure language
   - Placement and prominence requirements

9. **Produce compliance report:**

   ### Summary
   - Overall status: ✅ Compliant / 🟡 Needs fixes / 🔴 Do not publish
   - Critical issues: [count]
   - Fixes required before publication: [list]

   ### Flagged Claims
   For each problematic claim:
   - Original text
   - Issue identified
   - Jurisdictions affected
   - Recommended revision
   - Substantiation needed (if claim to be retained)

10. **Offer next steps:**
    > 1. **Rewrite** — I'll produce compliant alternative copy that preserves marketing intent
    > 2. **Substantiation checklist** — what evidence you need to support each claim
    > 3. **Disclaimer package** — required disclaimers/disclosures for all jurisdictions
    > 4. **Full campaign review** — review all materials in the campaign together
    > 5. **Regulatory filing** — if pre-clearance is required (health, financial)
    > 6. **Something else**
